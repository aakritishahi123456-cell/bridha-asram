Current Projects

Our ongoing initiatives focused on expanding our reach and improving the quality of care we provide.

Shelter Expansion Project

In Progress

Expanding our main shelter facility in Surkhet to accommodate 50 additional residents with improved amenities and accessibility features.

Progress 75%

Timeline: January 2024 - June 2024

Budget: NPR 2,500,000

Beneficiaries: 50 additional residents

Mobile Healthcare Unit

Active

Deploying mobile healthcare units to reach remote communities in Jajarkot district, providing medical care and health screenings.

Coverage 60%

Timeline: March 2024 - Ongoing

Budget: NPR 1,800,000

Beneficiaries: 500+ rural residents

Skills Training Program

Planning

Launching vocational training programs to help homeless individuals develop marketable skills and achieve self-sufficiency.

Development 25%

Timeline: May 2024 - December 2024

Budget: NPR 1,200,000

Beneficiaries: 100 trainees

Community Kitchen Project

Active

Establishing community kitchens to provide nutritious meals to vulnerable populations and create employment opportunities.

Implementation 90%

Timeline: February 2024 - April 2024

Budget: NPR 800,000

Beneficiaries: 200 daily meals

Completed Projects

Successfully completed initiatives that have made a lasting impact on our community.

Emergency Shelter Setup

Established our first emergency shelter facility in Surkhet, providing immediate accommodation for 45 individuals.

Completed: December 2023

Impact: 45 people housed

Healthcare Partnership

Partnered with local hospitals to provide regular health checkups and medical care for our residents.

Completed: January 2024

Impact: 150+ health checkups

Volunteer Training Program

Trained 42 volunteers in elderly care, first aid, and community outreach to strengthen our support network.

Completed: February 2024

Impact: 42 trained volunteers
