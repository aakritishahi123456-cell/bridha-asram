Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, volunteer opportunities, and how to get involved.

How can I volunteer?

Visit our volunteer page to learn about opportunities and fill out an application. We provide training for all volunteers.

What services do you provide?

We offer shelter, meals, healthcare, counseling, and job training for homeless and elderly individuals.

How can I make a donation?

You can donate online through our website, by bank transfer, or visit our facility in person.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes, we accept walk-ins for emergency situations. For non-emergency services, please call ahead to schedule.

What areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Surkhet district with our main facility and extend services to Jajarkot through mobile units.

How can organizations partner with you?

Contact us directly to discuss partnership opportunities. We welcome collaborations with other NGOs, businesses, and government agencies.
