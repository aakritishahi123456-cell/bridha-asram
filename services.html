Our Services

Comprehensive care and support programs designed to serve the most vulnerable members of our society.

Homeless Care Program

Providing shelter, meals, healthcare, and rehabilitation services to homeless individuals. We offer a safe haven and pathway to self-sufficiency.

Emergency shelter and accommodation
Daily meals and nutrition support
Healthcare and medical assistance
Job training and placement support
Counseling and rehabilitation services

Elderly Care Program

Comprehensive care for elderly individuals who need support, companionship, and medical attention. Ensuring dignity in their golden years.

24/7 care and supervision
Medical care and health monitoring
Social activities and companionship
Spiritual and emotional support
Family coordination and communication

Additional Support Services

Beyond our core programs, we offer various support services to ensure comprehensive care.

Healthcare Services

Regular health checkups, medication management, and emergency medical care.

  • • Regular health screenings
  • • Medication management
  • • Emergency medical response
  • • Specialist consultations

Nutrition Programs

Balanced meals and nutritional support tailored to individual needs.

  • • Three nutritious meals daily
  • • Special dietary accommodations
  • • Nutritional counseling
  • • Food distribution programs

Counseling & Mental Health

Emotional support and mental health services for overall wellbeing.

  • • Individual counseling sessions
  • • Group therapy programs
  • • Crisis intervention
  • • Mental health assessments

Skills Training

Vocational training and skill development programs for self-sufficiency.

  • • Vocational training programs
  • • Basic literacy classes
  • • Life skills workshops
  • • Job placement assistance

Community Outreach

Identifying and connecting with vulnerable individuals in the community.

  • • Street outreach programs
  • • Community awareness campaigns
  • • Mobile support units
  • • Partnership with local organizations

Emergency Response

24/7 emergency support and crisis intervention services.

  • • 24/7 emergency hotline
  • • Crisis intervention services
  • • Emergency shelter placement
  • • Disaster response support

Our primary service area with comprehensive facilities and programs.

Services Available:

  • • 24/7 care services
  • • Healthcare center
  • • Training programs
Coverage:
  • • Birendranagar
  • • Rural municipalities
  • • Remote villages
  • • Mobile outreach

Jajarkot District

Expanding services to reach vulnerable populations in rural areas.

Services Available:
  • • Mobile care units
  • • Community partnerships
  • • Emergency response
  • • Outreach programs
Coverage:
  • • Khalanga
  • • Rural communities
  • • Remote settlements
  • • Seasonal camps
