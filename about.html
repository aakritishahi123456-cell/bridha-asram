About Us

Learn about our mission, vision, and commitment to serving the most vulnerable members of our society.

Our Mission & Vision

Guided by compassion and driven by purpose, we work towards a Nepal where no one is left behind.

Our Mission

To provide dignity, shelter, care, and compassion to homeless and elderly individuals, ensuring a humane and respectful life for the most vulnerable members of society.

Our Vision

To build a compassionate Nepal where no elderly or homeless person is left abandoned, neglected, or without care.

Our Story

Founded with a deep commitment to social justice and human dignity, Buddhabhoomi Human Service Ashram began as a grassroots initiative to address the growing needs of homeless and elderly individuals in Nepal.

Our journey started in Surkhet, where we witnessed firsthand the challenges faced by the most vulnerable members of our society. What began as a small shelter has grown into a comprehensive care system that serves multiple communities across Nepal.

Today, we continue to expand our reach while maintaining our core values of compassion, dignity, and respect for every individual we serve.

Our Values

  • Compassion and Empathy
  • Human Dignity and Respect
  • Community Engagement
  • Transparency and Accountability
  • Sustainable Impact

Our Locations

We serve communities across Nepal, with primary operations in Surkhet and expanding to Jajarkot.

Surkhet (Primary)

Our main operations center, serving the homeless and elderly population in Birendranagar and surrounding areas.

  • • Main shelter facility
  • • 24/7 care services
  • • Community outreach programs
  • • Volunteer coordination center

Jajarkot (Secondary)

Expanding our services to reach more vulnerable populations in rural areas of Jajarkot district.

  • • Mobile care units
  • • Community partnerships
  • • Emergency response services
  • • Local volunteer network
